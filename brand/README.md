# Monkey & Bot Brand

## Overview
Monkey & Bot is a consulting services company focused on AI automation and digital transformation.

## The Story
**"I am the Monkey, AI is the Bot."**

Founded by a serial tech founder with nearly 20 years at the forefront of software development. Computer science educated, career spent building companies from writing code to building brands. AI has become the force multiplier - scaling one person's deep expertise into an agency-level operation.

## Target Audience
**SMBs** looking to:
- Leverage AI for automation and scaling
- Implement modern SEO strategies
- Build or refresh their brand
- Develop websites and custom applications
- Transform their digital presence

## Value Proposition

### Primary
- AI automation consulting
- AI integration and implementation

### Secondary (AI-augmented)
- Digital strategy & modern SEO
- Brand development
- Website design & development
- Custom application development
- Go-to-market (GTM) services

## Key Differentiator
All services are heavily augmented by AI - we don't just consult on AI, we use it to deliver faster, smarter, and more cost-effective results. One founder + AI = agency-level output.
